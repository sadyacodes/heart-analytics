 💓 Health Analytics - Heart Disease Risk Prediction Dashboard

🏥 AI-powered web application for heart health assessment and monitoring.

## ✨ Features

📝 **Quick Assessment** - 4-question health risk evaluation  
📊 **Visual Analytics** - Pie charts & bar graphs for risk visualization  
📜 **Prediction History** - Track all your past assessments  
📄 **Reports** - Download detailed health reports  
💡 **Recommendations** - Personalized lifestyle tips  
🩺 **Doctor Search** - Find cardiologists near you (for high-risk users)

| Page | Purpose |
|------|---------|
